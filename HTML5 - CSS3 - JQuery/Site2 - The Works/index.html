All about the Cosmos Pizza

Many years ago, in a small Italian village, on the south-west coast of Sicily, the very first Cosmo's Pizza was fired in a wood burning oven. The crust was crunchy, the cheese was organic, and the mouths were watering.

A bit more about Cosmo's Pizza

Cosmo was a quite man, with many important things on his mind. He considered every decision deeply. He worried about the future of his family, and of his community. Finally, he decided that the only way to protect the ones he loved, was to learn to make the finest pizza's the world have even seen.
